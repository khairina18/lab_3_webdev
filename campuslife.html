First-time Hiking

I just wanted to share my first ever experience hiking with my friends. Actually, we have a group project that we need do documentation and we decided to go to Bukit Botak, Bukit Berdiri and Bukit Payong at Parit Sulong, Batu Pahat, Johor. There were five of my friends who already know the hiking trails, so we just follow them and these three hills are connected with each other. The trail is not a beginner friendly but it was very fun to try it and I would definitely go with my friends again. The view on top of the hill is breathtaking and stunning! Not even in camera lens that could capture how beautiful the view was. I am very grateful to my team members who would always wait for me and help me whenever I am having difficulties. This is because I actually have asthma and that explains why I never get the chance to experience doing extreme activity before this.

One of my achievement that I am proud of and I am really glad that I join this marathon.
